Our three sites are pruned and like clockwork buds burst on the spring equinox. With such a wet winter here we are ready for a busy spring - undervine weeding, bud rubbing the trunks & shoot thining. We are now fully certified organic with BioGro (no. 5202). Bring on the sun.
