    Side Note

    We're set on making our wines
    true to the place they grow.
    The winemaking is simple -
    good estate grown fruit, organic
    & biodynamic, handpicked,
    vineyard derived yeasts, no
    addiivities or fining, minimal
    sulphur at bottling and where
    possible, no filtration. Then we
    have character.

    Our three sites are pruned and like clockwork buds burst on the spring equinox. With such a wet winter here we are ready for a busy spring - undervine weeding, bud rubbing the trunks & shoot thining. We are now fully certified organic with BioGro (no. 5202).

    Damsteep Pinot Noir 2015

    Deep ruby red hue. Crushed raspberry, red currant jelly, sandstone and white rock. Silken, rich and expansive fruit flow across the palate with savory underlying tannin giving tension and power. Flavors of dark clay with a strong mineral line that is saline and persistent give an impression of white rock and elevation. Drink now until 2026.
